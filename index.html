About Us

Litchies is designed to make your local shopping experience effortless, rewarding, and budget-friendly. Whether you’re visiting a big store or a small shop in Agra, our app ensures that you get exclusive discounts and exciting cashback on every purchase.

How to Earn Cashback

With a seamless app experience, you can discover, claim, and enjoy
exclusive deals in just a few taps.

Shop at Partner Store

Visit a shop that’s onboarded with Litchies.

Enter, Generate & Pay

Enter the amount, generate a QR code, and ask the shopkeeper to scan it.

Claim Your Offer

After shopping, go to the ‘Claim Offer’ screen in the app.

Scan & Pay

Get instant cashback in your wallet and redeem it for discounts.
