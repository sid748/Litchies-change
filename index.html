About Us

Litchies is designed to make your local shopping experience effortless, rewarding, and budget-friendly. Whether you’re visiting a big store or a small shop in Agra, our app ensures that you get exclusive discounts and exciting cashback on every purchase. Discover nearby shops, claim discounts, and earn cashback effortlessly—all through the Litchies app.

How to Earn Cashback
